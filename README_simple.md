# KeyCadence - For Kids

## What is KeyCadence?

Imagine you have a secret password. But what if someone bad guesses your password?

KeyCadence is a helper that watches **how you type**. Everyone types differently! Some people type fast. Some people type slow. Some people pause between certain letters.

KeyCadence remembers how YOU type. Then, if someone else tries to log in with your password, KeyCadence knows it's not you because they type differently!

It's like having a guard dog that recognizes your footsteps.

## How does it work?

### Step 1: Learn Your Typing (Enrollment)
When you first use the app, you type your password. KeyCadence watches how long you take between each letter.

For example, when you type "hello":
- h → e: 100 milliseconds
- e → l: 80 milliseconds  
- l → l: 60 milliseconds
- l → o: 90 milliseconds

KeyCadence writes this down. It's like learning your secret handshake.

### Step 2: Check Every Login (Verification)
Every time you log in, KeyCadence compares how you're typing NOW to how you USUALLY type.

If it matches → "Okay, that's you! Come in!"
If it doesn't match → "Hmm, this doesn't feel like you. Are you sure it's you?"

### Step 3: Make a Decision
If something feels wrong, KeyCadence asks for more proof. Maybe it asks you to type your password again, or sends a code to your phone.

## Why is this important?

Bad guys can steal your password. They can even steal your phone and get your special codes.

But they can't steal HOW YOU TYPE. That's yours alone!

KeyCadence protects you even when your password and codes are stolen.
